Corolla

Big houses, wide beaches, and the road runs out here.

28 miFrom Nags Head
4–7 BR (median 6)Typical size

Corolla is where the Outer Banks goes big — the median rental here is six bedrooms, and multi-family groups treat it as the default. With 2,618 rental properties it's the largest vacation inventory on the northern beaches, most of it built in planned oceanside communities off Route 12. It's roughly 28 miles from Nags Head, which means you commit: you're not day-tripping to the central beaches on a whim.

Best for

  • Large multi-family groups
  • Big-house amenities (pools, game rooms)
  • Lighthouse and wild-horse day trips
  • People who want beach, not boardwalk

Not ideal for

  • Couples or small groups
  • Anyone who wants to be central to the whole OBX
Access & character note: The paved road ends at the north end of Corolla — everything beyond (Carova) is 4WD beach driving only. The Currituck Beach Lighthouse is here and climbable in season.
